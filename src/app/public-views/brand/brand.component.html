Participating Brands

ToysRUs is relentlessly striving to be the best toy and baby retail company for the world. Founded in 1948 by Charles Lazarus, ToysRUs revolutionized quality, value and selection and realized that success meant focusing on the everyday shopper. It has solidified its position by offering a differentiated shopping experience through its family of brands. ToysRUs opened its first store in Bengaluru at Phoenix Market City in 2017.
