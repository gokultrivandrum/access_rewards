FAQs
  • What is the ACCESS REWARDS Program all about?

    • The ACCESS REWARDS Program is a loyalty program for customers of all Tablez Brands as below:

    • Cold Stone
    • ToysRUs
    • BabiesRUs
    • Springfield
    • Women'Secret
    • Galito's
    • Bloomsbury’s
  • What is special about the ACCESS REWARDS Program?
    • In ACCESS REWARDS, we reward our loyal customers with points on every purchase made at any Tablez brand stores. Additionally, we also run special offers & promotions.
  • How can a customer become a ACCESS REWARDS Program member?
    • To become an ACCESS REWARDS Member, you just need to be a customer at any of the Tablez brand outlets in India and register for ACCESS REWARDS Program
    • Submit the requested details to the store management.
    • On registration as an ACCESS REWARDS Program member, you will receive a welcome text message or an e-mail.
  • Is there any joining fee at the time of registration?
    • The membership is absolutely FREE. There is no joining fee.
  • What is the information required to become a ACCESS REWARDS Program member?
    • Name
    • Mobile phone number
    • Date of Birth
    • Anniversary Date
    • PIN CODE
    • Email address
    • Home Address
    • Child’s Name*
      • *applicable in ToysRUs & BabiesRUs stores only.
  • How do I earn points?
    • You will start earning 4 points for every Rs. 100 worth of purchase after registration
    • Once you enroll in the program and make your first purchase, you will also receive 150 BONUS points in addition to the normal points earned for your purchase
    • Please ensure that you quote your Mobile number to the cashier before billing to collect your points. You'll automatically earn points for all eligible transactions
    • Tax gratuity and other exclusions are not eligible
    • Please check at store how many points you will earn on every transaction
  • What is the value of each earned point in ACCESS REWARDS Program?
    • One ACCESS REWARDS Point is equivalent to INR 1.00
  • When will the earned points be credited to the ACCESS REWARDS Program?
    • The points will be credited to your account within 24 hours.
    • If the points are not credited within 24 hours, please call and quote your bill number.
  • I haven’t been credited my ACCESS REWARDS Program points for the purchase I made. What do I do?
    • If you have not received the points, kindly escalate the matter to the store manager within 48hrs. Please carry a physical copy of the bill for perusal of store management.
    • The store management will ensure your valuable points are updated.
  • How do I redeem my accumulated points in ACCESS REWARDS Program?
    • You provide your mobile number registered with ACCESS Rewards Program to the store cashier to redeem your points.
    • Redeem anytime & any number of points from the available point balance in your ACCESS Rewards account. You can check your balance at any of our brand stores.
  • Is there a validity period for the ACCESS REWARDS Program points?
    • Yes. The ACCESS Rewards points will be valid for 1 year from date of issue.
  • How can I check my ACCESS REWARDS Program points balance?
    • You can check your point balance at any of the Tablez group brand stores in India.
  • My mobile phone number has changed, what do I need to do continue with the ACCESS REWARDS Program?
    • For verification purposes, please provide a valid proof of identity with photo - Aadhaar Card, Driving License, PAN Card - in person. The amendment will take 48 hours after verification is done.
  • I am a member without a valid mobile number and do not have proof to claim I am an existing ACCESS REWARDS Program member?
    • For customers enrolled with an invalid mobile number and unverifiable in any way, we will register you as a new customer. Please fill up the enrollment form to start the new ACCESS REWARDS account.

All points in any ACCESS REWARDS Program account inactive for 12 months will expire, and all points in an account terminated by TABLEZ will immediately expire upon such termination. Points obtained through special promotional offers may have additional limitations, including limited time periods for you to use such points and/or accelerated expiration schedules. All other points have a rolling 12- month validity where in points earned in a month will expire 12 months from month of earning.
